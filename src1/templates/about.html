About Hotel BT

Hotel BT stands on the edge of lake Dayem viewing the boats that sail pass as well as the birds that fly by. We have the cleanest of rooms and the fanciest of foods.

Our staff goes through months of training before being able to even enter the building. Every couple hours one of our janitors will come to clean your room.
